# Governance Framework

## Governance tiers
### Tier 0:
Planning only

### Tier 1:
Read-only research and setup

### Tier 2:
Draft-only content and staging

### Tier 3:
Publishing with approval

### Tier 4:
Autonomous publishing only after validation

## Kill rules
Pause or terminate brands if:
- No traction after review window
- Poor monetization path
- Excessive operational burden
- Platform penalties

## Weekly review
Every project must be reviewed for:
- Traffic
- Engagement
- Monetization
- Risk
- Automation quality
