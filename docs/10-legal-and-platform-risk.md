# Legal and Platform Risk Framework

## Purpose
Reduce avoidable bans, compliance issues, monetization failures, and reputational damage.

## Required baseline
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- Cookie policy where relevant
- Affiliate disclosures
- AI disclosure where appropriate

## Platform safety
Avoid:
- Spam automation
- Fake engagement
- Black-hat SEO
- Mass duplicate content
- Copyright abuse
- Policy circumvention

## Social safety
Each platform should have:
- Posting cadence controls
- Content quality checks
- Human review for sensitive topics
- Account recovery plan

### Per-platform policy
- **X (Twitter)** — `docs/x-platform-risk.md` is the canonical governance doc.
  Treats X as a hostile platform: rate-limit assumptions, shadowban
  controls, link-placement rules, human review gates, brand-safe cadence
  ceilings, scheduling governance, escalation triggers. Mandatory reading
  for any X-facing PR.
- **TikTok / Instagram / Reddit / YouTube** — governed by the lab-wide
  posture above and the per-brand profile docs; X-tier governance docs
  to be added if needed.

## SEO safety
Prioritize:
- Originality
- Usefulness
- Brand clarity
- Accuracy
- Real value

## Monetization safety
Review policies before:
- Affiliate signup
- Ad network signup
- Sponsored content
- Lead gen

## Escalation triggers
Manual review required for:
- Health claims
- Finance claims
- Legal claims
- AI-generated controversial content
