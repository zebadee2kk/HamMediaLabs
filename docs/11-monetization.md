# Monetization Framework

## Monetization maturity model

### Stage 1 — Validation
Goal:
Traffic + audience proof

### Stage 2 — Capture
Goal:
Email + community + retargeting

### Stage 3 — Monetize
Examples:
- Affiliate
- Display ads
- Sponsorship
- Digital products
- Lead generation

### Stage 4 — Scale
Examples:
- Premium content
- Membership
- Brand partnerships
- Franchise / resale

## Monetization criteria
Only monetize aggressively if:
- Traffic quality exists
- Audience trust exists
- Conversion path exists
- Platform safety exists

## Default monetization tests
- Affiliate links
- Lead magnets
- Newsletter
- Low-friction digital assets

## Risk
Avoid over-monetizing too early if trust is not established.
