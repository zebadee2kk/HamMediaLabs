# Brand A — AI Escape (profile)

## Identity
- **Brand name:** AI Escape *(working title; verify domain availability before lock)*
- **Niche:** Applied AI for working professionals — using AI to claw back time, escape inefficient work, and build leverage.
- **Audience:** Knowledge workers (engineers, ops, marketers, consultants) aged 25–45 who already use AI casually and want to use it like a craftsperson.
- **Tone:** Confident peer who has actually shipped with these tools. Not a guru.
- **Core promise:** *"What I actually use, what I dropped, what saved me real time."*

## Business hypothesis
- **Why this niche?** Saturation at the top is a function of "tool of the week" content, not applied-workflow content. The opening is *workflows* (durable) instead of *tools* (volatile).
- **Monetisation path:** SaaS affiliate (recurring) + £29 digital templates pack + newsletter sponsorship at scale.
- **Primary channel:** SEO blog.
- **Secondary channel:** X (commentary + distribution).

## KPI baseline (90-day)
- **Traffic target:** 2,500 sessions/month by day 90.
- **Engagement target:** ≥30% returning visitor rate; ≥1.5 pages/session.
- **Conversion target:** 100 newsletter subscribers by day 90.
- **Review window:** Weekly from launch + day-60 and day-90 milestones.

## Kill / hold / scale
- **Kill threshold:** Brand Score (`docs/PROJECTHYDRA-MASTER-PLAN.md` §13.2) <0.30 for 2 consecutive weeks after week 6, AND no monetisation signal.
- **Hold threshold:** 0.30 ≤ BS < 0.55.
- **Scale trigger:** BS ≥0.55 AND newsletter signups ≥30/week sustained for 4 weeks AND first £100 affiliate revenue.

## Operational
- **Parent account:** hammedialabs.master (Gmail), GitHub org, Cloudflare account.
- **Brand child accounts:**
  - `aiescape.editorial@…` (Gmail alias)
  - `aiescape.social.x@…` (X account)
  - `aiescape.social.yt@…` (YouTube Shorts experiment from M2)
  - Newsletter list (Buttondown free → beehiiv if it crosses 100 subs and we want to use it as a product)
- **CMS:** Astro static (uses `brands/templates/site/`)
- **Socials:** X (manual posting), YouTube Shorts (manual)
- **Providers:** Cloudflare Pages (host), Supabase HQ (telemetry), Plausible/CF Analytics, Gemini + Groq + OpenRouter (via HQ router)

## Editorial content plan (first 90 days)
- 5 cornerstone pieces published in weeks 1–4 (one per week).
- 2 sidebar/secondary pieces per week from week 4 onward.
- Newsletter from week 1 (weekly cadence, paired with publish day).
- X cadence: 5 posts/week, one of them deliberately spicy/contrarian.

## Tier path
- Phase 2: T2 (draft + manual publish).
- Day 30+: T3 candidate if quality bar holds.
- T4: blocked all year.

## Notes
