# Brand B — Corporate Satire (profile)

## Identity
- **Brand name:** *TBD* — working title "Corporate Theatre" / "Quarter Plan" / "OK-Aren't". Pick one in Phase 1.
- **Niche:** Satire of corporate / consulting / tech-job culture: meetings, OKRs, all-hands, performance reviews, agile theatre.
- **Audience:** Tech / consulting / financial-services workers, 25–45, on TikTok and Instagram, with crossover from r/consulting, r/cscareerquestions, LinkedIn (ironic).
- **Tone:** Dry, observational, never punching down. Recognisable specificity ("the Tuesday 10:30 sync").
- **Core promise:** "If you've sat through it, you'll laugh at it."

## Business hypothesis
- **Why this niche?** High engagement potential, easy production cadence, durable format space (corporate culture isn't going anywhere). Validation-only first.
- **Monetisation path:** Validation → audience → optional merch (POD) → inbound sponsorships. No outbound sponsorship pitches in year 1.
- **Primary channel:** TikTok.
- **Secondary channel:** Instagram Reels.

## KPI baseline (90-day)
- **Traffic target:** N/A (audience-only).
- **Engagement target:** ≥3% engagement rate on TikTok by day 60; ≥5,000 followers across primary+mirror by day 90.
- **Conversion target:** N/A in year 1.
- **Review window:** Weekly.

## Kill / hold / scale
- **Kill threshold:** No clip exceeds 5,000 views by week 6 AND no follower acceleration (>50/week sustained).
- **Hold threshold:** Mixed signal, no platform strikes, low operational burden.
- **Scale trigger:** ≥10,000 followers and a recognisable repeatable bit; ≥2 inbound sponsorship inquiries; consider merch.

## Tier path
- T2 manual posting only. T3 considered after 90 days of clean operation.
- T4: blocked.

## Risk-specific notes
- Highest ban risk in the portfolio. Posting cadence cap: 5/week per platform. No automation. Account-recovery plan documented before first post.
- EU AI Act satire carve-out: disclosure of AI use is required even for satire; we comply by visible labels in bio, pinned post, video first 3 seconds, and About page.
- Never synthesise real public figures.
- Never punch down (e.g. junior staff, support staff, women, minorities). Punches go up (executives, consultants, the operator themselves).
