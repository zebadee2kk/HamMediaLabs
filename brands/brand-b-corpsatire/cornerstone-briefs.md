# Brand B — Cornerstone Content Briefs (first 5 series-anchors)

> For Brand B, "cornerstone" = a recurring **series** the channel can rely on, not a single blog post.
> Each entry below defines a series; the first episode is the brief.

---

## Series 1 — *Translations*
- **Format:** 18–24s talking-head + text overlay.
- **Episode 1 brief:** 5 consultant phrases, each shown as a chat bubble; voiceover gives the plain-English translation. E.g. "Let's take this offline" → "I'm losing this argument; please stop talking".
- **Cadence:** 1/week.
- **Variants:** *Translations: Engineering*, *Translations: Sales*, *Translations: HR*.

## Series 2 — *Calendar Reads*
- **Format:** First-person screen-capture of a Monday-morning calendar, opening invitations one at a time, reading the subject line, then the actual one-line reaction.
- **Episode 1 brief:** Six fictional invites: 8:30 "Brief Sync", 9:00 "Pre-call for the call", 10:30 "Brad's OKR Refresh (FINAL v3)", 11:30 "Optional: Lunch & Learn", 13:00 "RACI Walkthrough", 15:00 "Performance Review (Don't be late this time)".
- **Cadence:** 1/week. Sometimes themed (interview-loop Monday; layoff Monday handled with care or skipped).

## Series 3 — *Slack History*
- **Format:** Faux Slack thread, four messages, animated to appear one at a time. Voiceover reads each.
- **Episode 1 brief:** "Tom: Quick Q on the deck. // Tom: It's about the timeline. // Tom: Actually nvm. // Tom: Sorry one more. // (12 hours later) Tom: still chewing on this 🙇"
- **Cadence:** 1/week.

## Series 4 — *1:1 Bingo*
- **Format:** A bingo card on screen; voiceover lists beats; squares fill in as they happen.
- **Episode 1 brief:** Bingo card with: "How are you, *really?*", "Let's level-set", "I want to be transparent…", "You're an A-player", "Have you read [book]?", "Career conversations on the radar".
- **Cadence:** 1/week.

## Series 5 — *The Framework*
- **Format:** 30–45s; protagonist invents a framework on the fly to win a meeting. On-screen scribble of a 2×2 with absurd axes.
- **Episode 1 brief:** "Effort × Impact" but the axes are "Vibes" and "Whose Fault It Is If It Fails".
- **Cadence:** every other week.

---

## Production discipline

- **Cadence ceiling:** total 5 posts/week across all series and Reels mirror.
- **Editing time:** ≤45 min/episode at MVP.
- **QA:** one editor pass for tone (banned-topic check), one for compliance (AI label in first 3s + caption tag + bio match).
- **Posting:** manual; from the operator's device; no scheduled automation.
- **Mirroring:** post natively to Instagram Reels within 24h; do not cross-post the TikTok watermark.
- **Comment moderation:** twice daily for 14 days post-publish; pin a friendly comment seed on each.
