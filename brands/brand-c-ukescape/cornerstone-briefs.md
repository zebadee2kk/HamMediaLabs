## Cornerstone 1 — "The April 2026 price cap, in plain English"

- **Slug:** `/april-2026-price-cap-explained`
- **Primary intent:** explainer; news-peg evergreen.
- **Target queries:** April 2026 energy price cap; Ofgem price cap typical bill 2026
- **Length:** 1,400–1,800 words.
- **Angle:** What the cap is, what it isn't, who it covers, what it means for *your* bill (range, not point).
- **Structure:**
  1. TL;DR table (typical bill, change vs. last cap, what's coming in July).
  2. What the cap actually caps (unit rate + standing charge).
  3. How to translate the cap to your actual bill.
  4. What we'd watch this quarter (forecast inputs, not predictions).
  5. Links: Ofgem cap page, MoneyHelper, our switching guide.
- **Affiliate placement:** *optional* link to an FCA-compatible comparison-site partner programme **after** a thorough info section; no inline "click here to save" copy.
- **Compliance:** FCA disclaimer block, AI label, sources cited inline.

- **Compliance:** FCA disclaimer; "this is information, not advice"; charity links for those in debt.
