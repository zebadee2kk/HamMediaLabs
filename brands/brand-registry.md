# Master Brand Registry

## Purpose
Single source of truth for all HamMediaLabs brands.

## Fields
- Brand name
- Niche
- Status (Planning / Active / Hold / Kill / Scale)
- Launch date
- Monetization model
- Primary channel
- Secondary channel
- Monthly cost
- Estimated revenue
- KPI score
- Kill threshold
- Scale trigger
- Notes

## Rule
Every brand must exist here before launch.
