# Kill / Hold / Scale Review

## Kill if:
- Weak growth
- Poor monetization path
- High effort / low return
- Platform risk

## Hold if:
- Mixed data
- Low operational burden
- Some upside remains

## Scale if:
- Strong repeatable traffic
- Strong conversion
- Defensible niche
- Low operational complexity

## Scale actions
- Increase publishing
- Upgrade infrastructure
- Expand monetization
- Consider dedicated node

## Financial thresholds (per `docs/profit-model.md` §6)
- **Kill (financial):** attributed monthly cost > attributed monthly revenue for 6 consecutive months AND `brand_score` <0.30 over the same window AND no monetisation in flight. Brand B audience-only exception in Phases A–B.
- **Hold:** revenue covers cost within ±50% with stable trend.
- **Scale:** Stage 3 monetisation criteria met AND 12-month projected revenue ≥3× annualised infra + content cost.

Every verdict files a decision-log entry per the standard template.
