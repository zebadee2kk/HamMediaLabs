# Kill / Hold / Scale Review

## Kill if:
- Weak growth
- Poor monetization path
- High effort / low return
- Platform risk

## Hold if:
- Mixed data
- Low operational burden
- Some upside remains

## Scale if:
- Strong repeatable traffic
- Strong conversion
- Defensible niche
- Low operational complexity

## Scale actions
- Increase publishing
- Upgrade infrastructure
- Expand monetization
- Consider dedicated node
